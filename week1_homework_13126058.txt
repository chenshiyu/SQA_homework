	In my personal understanding, software quality can be summarized as the degree of consensus of software and the explicit and implicit requirements. Software quality assurance is refers to in order to help the software can meet the demand of explicit and implicit in a series of activities.
	Perfect software quality assurance activities should be present throughout the project. It will participate in establishing plans, standards and procedures at the beginning of the project. Software quality assurance department reviews and audits to verify whether the software is up to standard and these activities will be through all the phase of the software products such as requirements analysis, design, code and test which is the activity existed in almost every project.
